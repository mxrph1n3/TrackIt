**Description:**

TrackIt is your daily command center: plan tasks, train with structured programs, log meals and water, track spending, and build habits — all synced in the cloud.

**What you can do**
- Planner with tasks, subtasks, and focus sessions
- 100+ workout programs plus custom programs (Pro)
- Nutrition targets from your profile (BMR/TDEE estimates)
- Finance overview, subscriptions, and insights
- Habits, journal, and gamification (XP, levels, leaderboard)
- AI Coach (Pro) — personalized summary from your recent activity

**TrackIt Pro**
- Monthly or yearly subscription with a 7-day free trial
- AI Coach, advanced analytics, premium themes, full program library
- Manage or cancel anytime in device Settings → Subscriptions

Health and AI features are for general wellness only — not medical or financial advice.
